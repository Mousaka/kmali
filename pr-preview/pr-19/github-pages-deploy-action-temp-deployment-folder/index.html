Katja Mali is a landscape architect with a passion for creating spaces that connect people with the natural world. Her work spans private gardens, public parks, and urban environments across Scandinavia.

With a careful eye for ecology, light, and materiality, Katja brings a thoughtful and site-specific approach to every project — from intimate courtyards to large-scale public landscapes.
